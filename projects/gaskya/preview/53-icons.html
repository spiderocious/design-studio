The rule

Line icons, single weight, drawn to the same 24-grid, stroked in ink (or aubergine when interactive). No filled icons except inside a filled button. No emoji as icons — this is a serious instrument that respects its user, even when it's being kind. The decorative ◆ used as a section glyph is the one ornamental mark, borrowed from the “generated/verified” idea of a cut, faceted thing.
