Principles

  • Snappy, not slow. Anything over 400ms feels sluggish in a party game.
  • Spring for celebration only. Page transitions, hover, and ambient UI use ease-default.
  • No punitive motion. Wrong answer flashes; it does not shake. The game encourages, never punishes.
  • Reduced motion respected. Every non-essential animation gates behind prefers-reduced-motion and degrades to fade or no-motion.
