Selection

The selection primitives are how a host configures a game. They appear inside config screens (Word Bomb, Plead Your Case) and inside category filter chips on the catalogue. Segmented for 2–3 mutually exclusive choices; chips for multi-select; radio cards for big consequential picks; switches for binary on/off.
