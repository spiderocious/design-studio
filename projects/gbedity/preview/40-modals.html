Surface · Modals · including the CRITICAL idiom

Modals lift on a tinted-mint scrim. Three intents: standard (a confirmation, a quick decision), destructive (red action button, no special type-to-confirm — for reversible-but-loud actions), and CRITICAL (irreversible — booting a player, ending the session mid-game, voiding a round). The CRITICAL idiom requires typing the literal word to confirm — colour alone never carries the weight.
