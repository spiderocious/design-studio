Numerals

Every number in Gbedity is set in Fraunces SemiBold with tabular numerals. The brief is explicit: numbers shout in this product — vitals shout, scores shout, the bomb timer shouts. Three roles dominate: timer (huge, tinted by urgency), score (large, accent on win), and game ID (medium, tinted to category at 55% opacity).
