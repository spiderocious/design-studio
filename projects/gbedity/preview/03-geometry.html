Spacing & geometry

Nothing is sharp. Every interactive element, every surface, every illustration carries rounded geometry — buttons start at 16px, cards at 20px, the stage frame at 32px. Spacing is on an 8px grid, generous. Tight is wrong. Depth comes from layered colour surfaces, not drop shadows.
