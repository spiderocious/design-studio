Buttons

Three intents — primary (forward motion), secondary (alternative), ghost (low-emphasis) — plus two reserved variants: celebrate for accent-orange moments and danger for destructive actions. Three sizes. Nunito 700 everywhere; Fraunces never on a button.

Primary — Action Green

Secondary — outlined

Ghost — tertiary

Celebrate — accent orange (reserved)

Used only on celebration moments — score callouts, winner reveals. Never as a generic accent.

Danger — tomato red (reserved)

Boot a player, end a session, void an action. The CRITICAL modal idiom (see overlays) requires more than just colour — typing or hold-to-confirm.

On the stage frame — primary stays white

In context — config screen footer

Primary leads. Secondary is the calm escape. Ghost only when both are too loud.
