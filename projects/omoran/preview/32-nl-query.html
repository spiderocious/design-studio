✦“Where do mobile users drop off in the checkout funnel?”

Mobile users hold steady through cart, then fall off hardest at the payment step — only 38% who reach payment complete it, against 71% on desktop. That single step accounts for most of this week's −18% in completions.

↳ this is the query I ran read-only
SELECT event, count(DISTINCT distinct_id) AS users FROM events WHERE event IN ('view_cart','begin_checkout','add_payment','checkout_completed') AND properties.device = 'mobile' AND timestamp > now() - 7 GROUP BY event ORDER BY users DESC
