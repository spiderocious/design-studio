Mobile checkout slipped — and organic quietly carried the week.

Acme · Production · Mon 19 May 2026 · week of May 12–18

Activity held up — weekly active users rose +6% to 38,204, and total event volume crossed half a million. But two stories sit under that calm surface: a sharp, mobile-only dip in checkout, and a steady shift toward organic acquisition that's worth leaning into.

§2 The thing to look at first

Checkout completions fell 18% week-over-week, and almost all of it is Safari on iOS — desktop and Android were flat. The drop accelerated Thursday and held through the weekend, which rules out a one-off spike. It looks like a platform-specific break, not a demand problem.

Two-thirds of the lost completions reached the payment step and stopped there — so the funnel is intact up to checkout, then breaks.

§3 The quiet win

Organic search overtook direct as your top signup source this week and now converts to paid at 4.1× the rate of paid social. If that holds, shifting spend away from paid social wouldn't dent acquisition — it might improve it.
