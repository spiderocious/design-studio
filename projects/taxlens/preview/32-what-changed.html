Why — the three changes that move your number

Six other reform points exist; none change your result, so we leave them out.

01
Your first ₦800,000 is now completely tax-free
Under the old rules, tax began biting at ₦300,000. Raising the exempt threshold to ₦800,000 alone removes around ₦56,000 from what you used to owe.
NTA 2025 · Fourth Schedule
02
Rent relief replaced the old Consolidated Relief Allowance
The CRA is abolished. Instead you deduct 20% of rent, capped at ₦500,000. On your ₦1.8m rent that’s a ₦360,000 deduction — simpler to claim, and it needs documentation.
NTA 2025 · Fourth Schedule (reliefs)
03
Lower rates across the range you actually earn in
The slice from ₦800k to ₦3m is taxed at a flat 15%, replacing a stack of 7–19% mini-bands. That smoothing is where most of your ₦128,400 saving comes from.
